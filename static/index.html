What is GRAFF?

GRAFF is an AI-powered system that transforms educational textbook chapters into structured, searchable knowledge. It extracts every atomic fact from your content and organizes it for optimal learning.

2-Phase Analysis Pipeline

1

Structure Analysis

Identifies chapter sections, entities, keywords, and creates a comprehensive summary

2

Deep Extraction

Extracts ALL atomic facts (propositions) and synthesizes key takeaways. Each fact is tagged with Bloom's Taxonomy level for cognitive depth.

Processing Time: 15-30 Minutes
